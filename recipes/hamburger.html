The Classic Burger

Intro

Sink your teeth into a delicious restaurant-style, hamburger recipe made from lean beef. Skip the prepackaged patties and take the extra time to craft up your own, and that little extra effort will be worth it. To make cheeseburgers, about 1 minute before burgers are done, top with sliced cheese; continue cooking until cheese begins to melt.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground lean (7% fat) beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 minced onion
  • 1 or 2 gloves garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt and pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of iceberg lettuce
  • 4 thin slices red onion

Steps

  1. Mix beef, egg, garlic and onion in a large bowl
  2. Divide mixture into 4 equal amounts
  3. Shape and lay burgers on a grill over bed of hot coals or high heat on a gas grill.
  4. Cook burgers turning only once until browned on both sides
  5. Lay buns cut side down on grill and cook until toasted
  6. Assemble burger and add toppings as desired
